Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man of India

Scientist, teacher, and visionary leader who inspired millions of young minds with his thoughts, humility, and dedication to nation-building.

Early Life

Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, Dr. Kalam came from a humble background. His early struggles taught him discipline, honesty, and hard work.

Scientific Career

Working with ISRO and DRDO, Dr. Kalam led key missile projects that strengthened India's defense capabilities and earned him the title “Missile Man of India.”

People’s President

As the 11th President of India, he connected deeply with citizens and inspired students to dream big and work with integrity.

“Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action.”
